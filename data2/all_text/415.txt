留学生义教 - iTeach Program
2016春，来自哥伦比亚、克罗地亚、赞比亚、斐济、荷兰、德国、埃塞俄比亚、老挝和越南的9名留学生志愿者，参加了ICES与上海建青实验学校（小学部）共同策划组织的留学生义教活动。

In this spring semester, 9 international student volunteers from Columbia, Croatia, Zambia, Fiji, Netherlands, German, Ethiopia, Laos and Vietnam participated in the iTeach voluntary teaching program in Shanghai Jianqing Experimental School.
