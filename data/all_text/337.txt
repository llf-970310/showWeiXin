2016年3月11日，大赛组委会组织专家对来自全国27所高校的众多作品进行评审，最终选出入围作品34组。这些作品充分展现了留学生眼中的“中国元素”和“丝绸之路”。3月28日，大赛将进入微信平台大众投票环节，请大家拭目以待！
On March 11, 2016, the contest organizing committee invited experts to review the numerous works submitted from 27 high education institutes and put 34 excellent works on a short list. These works fully expressed the Chinese elements and the Silk Road from the perspective of international students. The public voting will be launched on WeChat platform from March 28. Stay tuned!


微信投票环节结束后，票数最高的前三组作品，将获得大赛“最佳人气奖”，颁发奖品及获奖证书。
Upon the result of Wechat voting, the winners receiving the top 3 most votes will be issued ‘Popularity Award’, prizes and certificates.
