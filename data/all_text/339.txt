 青岛四日游      
 Qingdao 4-Day Tour


May 5, Thursday
下午14:00延安路校区内集合（松江校区学生出发时间、地点另行通知），乘车前往上海虹桥火车站，乘G234次高铁前往青岛。抵达后入住酒店。 (晚餐自理)
Gather at Yan’an Road Campus before 14:00 pm (Songjiang Campus: departure time & place will be announced later). 
Take bus to Shanghai Hongqiao Railway Station and take No. G234 train to Qingdao. Check in at the hotel. (No meals provided, please prepare by yourself)


May 6, Friday
早餐后，攀登崂山太清宫风景区。午餐后，游览东海路雕塑一条街、石老人黄金海岸自由活动。游览青岛新城市的标志―五四广场、音乐广场。(含早餐、中餐)
After breakfast, visit Taiqing Temple of Laoshan.
After lunch (free time), enjoy art street of sculpture in Donghai Road and visit Gold Coast in Shilaoren tourist resort; visit the new landmark of Qingdao―May-Fourth and Music Square. 
(Breakfast & lunch included in fee)


May 7, Saturday
早餐后，游览青岛的百年标志―栈桥。午餐后，游览爱情庄园、万国建筑博览园---八大关 。(含早餐、中餐)
After breakfast, visit the historic landmark of Qingdao―Trestle Bridge.
After lunch, visit the Love Manor and the Architecture Expo Park―Badaguan.
 (Breakfast & dinner included in fee)


May 8, Sunday
早餐后，参观青岛啤酒博物馆。午餐后，乘G228次高铁返回上海。(含早餐、中餐)
After breakfast, visit Qingdao Beer Museum. 
After lunch, take No.G228 train back to Shanghai.
 (Breakfast & lunch included in fee)
