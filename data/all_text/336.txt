“Visiting Chinese Enterprises Program”
 
Fieldtrip to Allbright Law Office


March 10th 2016, a fieldtrip of “Visiting Chinese Enterprises Program” was organized by Donghua University International Culture Exchange School. 34 international students visited Allbright Law Offices being located in the Shanghai Tower which is the tallest building in Shanghai. 

Students and lawyers discussed about investment chances and relevant law affairs under “The Belt and Road Initiative” new international development strategy. This fieldtrip fully demonstrated a presence of enterprises in China and broaden students' horizon to China and the world.
